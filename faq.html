Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tallow products

What is Our Tallow Made From?

The best tallow is made from the rich, 'Leaf fat' or 'Suet' which means the fat that directly surrounds the liver and kidney organs.

It's the best fat to use because it's rich in 'skin-food' nutrients and minerals and is 'soft' by nature. Our beef-fat is exceptional quality because it is derived from Organically-raised, 100% Grass fed cattle, from our own farm and from like-minded ethical farmers we know & trust.

We process the fat at low temperature, and purify it 4x in spring water to preserve its innate nutrients and ensure the end product you receive is of high quality & pure.

What's Added to the Leaf Fat?

For Frankincense & Lavender, Scent-Free & Cedarwood Essential Oil Blends:

We add a small amount of locally sourced Organic, extra virgin cold-pressed olive oil.

For Vanilla Essential Oil Blend:

We add Organic cold-pressed almond oil.

Why Choose Tallow Over Other Moisturizers?

Organic, Grass-fed Tallow is the perfect all-in-one moisturizer for both face and body. Its unique composition is loaded with fat-soluble nutrients which are bio-compatible with human skin, meaning it mimics the structure of our own skin cells.

This allows Tallow to absorb deeply and deliver long-lasting nourishment — naturally.

"I used to use four skincare products twice a day… now I only use Tallow, and my skin has never been better."

Is Your Tallow Organic?

Yes! Our tallow comes from 100% grass-fed, organically-raised cattle from our own farm and trusted local suppliers. We never use cattle that have been given hormones, antibiotics, or chemical inputs.

All our additional ingredients (olive oil, almond oil, essential oils) are also organic and locally sourced whenever possible.

Will Tallow Clog My Pores?

No! Because tallow is bio-compatible with human skin (it closely mimics our skin's natural oils), it absorbs easily without clogging pores. Many customers with acne-prone skin find that tallow actually helps balance their skin.

Does It Have a Smell?

Our purification process removes the natural tallow scent, creating an odorless base. Our scent-free version has no added fragrance at all.

Our scented versions have subtle, natural aromas from organic essential oils (lavender, frankincense, cedarwood, or vanilla).

How Do I Use Tallow Balm?

Use it just like any moisturizer! A little goes a long way. Warm a small amount between your fingers and apply to clean, slightly damp skin on your face and/or body.

It can be used morning and night, and is suitable for all skin types.

How Should I Store My Tallow Balm?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Tallow is stable at room temperature but may soften in warm weather. If it gets too soft, simply refrigerate briefly to firm it up.

Is Tallow Suitable for Sensitive Skin?

Yes! Many people with sensitive skin find tallow to be gentler than conventional moisturizers because it contains no harsh chemicals, preservatives, or synthetic ingredients.

Our scent-free option is particularly good for very sensitive skin.

What's Your Farming Practice?

We use rotational grazing systems on our 100-acre farm in Gippsland, Victoria. This allows the soil to regenerate, increases biodiversity, and improves pasture quality while supporting healthy cattle and local ecosystems.

We honor our cattle by applying the 'nose-to-tail' philosophy of no waste.
