Our Story

From our farm to your skin

Farm Image

Our Tallow is Sourced From Our Own, and Local Like-Minded Farms

Our 100% Grass-fed, chemical-free cattle are raised using ethical and sustainable farming practices.

Using mineral rich, spring water, and organic local ingredients, we handcraft our Tallow balms in small batches at a time on-site.

Our Ethos

At Chantilly's Tallow we are passionate about living an organic healthy lifestyle. It's always been important to our family to know where our food is coming from, produce our own and support local organic ethical farmers.

With passions in permaculture and regeneration, raising cattle ourselves came as a natural progression, for food we could trust and to support the eco systems on our degraded 100 acre farm in Gippsland Victoria.

By moving our cattle through a rotational grazing system, we allow the soil time to regenerate, increase its bio-diversity & pasture quality in less land than traditional farming requires. We can support more cattle of higher quality, whilst offering a variety of creatures above and below the soil the opportunity to thrive.

By farming this way & crafting Tallow balms with pure ingredients YOU can trust we feel aligned with traditional healthy living practices of our forefathers, birthing a piece of the past into the now for future generations.
