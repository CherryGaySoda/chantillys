How Our Tallow is Made

With Love, By Hand

This part never gets old. Watching it come together, filling the jars/tins with soft, fragrant goodness, and hearing your stories of how much you love it. That's why we keep making it!

1

Fresh from the Source

We start with the freshest leaf fat, delivered the same day it's harvested.

2

Render with Care

The fat is chopped, grated, or minced, then slowly rendered using pure spring water that we hand-collect from a nearby natural spring.

3

4x Rendered

This careful rendering process is repeated four times until the fat becomes fully liquefied.

4

Filtered to Perfection

The liquid tallow is poured through a double-layered cheesecloth to remove impurities.

5

Settling Stage

Once set, the oil and water separate. We drain off the water and gently scrape away any remaining sediment.

6

Do It All Again

Yes, we repeat the process four times to achieve an ultra-clean, creamy, odourless tallow.

7

Blended with Goodness

The final tallow is reheated gently, then blended with nourishing oils like olive or almond, and infused with essential oils.

8

Let It Rest

We allow the blend to reset… tic toc tic toc.

9

Whipped to Perfection

Finally, we whip it into a silky, luxurious cream that looks good enough to eat — especially the vanilla-scented one!

Our Commitment to Quality

Low Temperature Processing

We process the fat at low temperatures to preserve its innate nutrients and skin-loving properties.

Purified 4 Times

We purify the tallow 4 times in spring water to ensure the end product you receive is of the highest quality and purity.

Small Batch Crafting

Every batch is made by hand in small quantities, ensuring attention to detail and consistent quality.

Fresh Ingredients

We use only the freshest leaf fat from grass-fed cattle and locally sourced organic oils.
